Welcome

Fractal Analytics presents an end to end Retail Shelf Management solution.

Forget the clumsiness of handling huge inventory details, unreliability of manual inspection and vague figures that do not reflect the actual market shares. Our solution is a rich hassle free experience, with completely automated pipeline, designed to eliminate any human error and make compliance montioring as easy as taking a picture.

Process Flow Introduction

  • Image Capturing

    At the store by the Sales Rep.

    The sales representative will take slightly pictures of the store shelves and upload them onto our servers.

    Our algorithm doesn't put any restriction on the order of images to be captured and evaluates the Shelf Share by adaptively figuring out the proper shelf image ordering. This is done at the next stage.

  • Image Processing & Stitching

    Our algorithm figures out the exact original shelf arrangement for the brands by performing image stitching on all the possible combinations of the arrangments of the input images.

  • Shelf Share Calculation

    The processed outputs are then considered as separate shelves. We detect the product boxes with high precision and classify the varients with an accuracy above 95%.

    The box locations are then used to determine the relative positions of independent shelves within the image and then evaluates several metrics like Shelf Facings per brand, Shelf Share, Position Maps, Out of Stock, etc.

  • Scores & Compliance Display

    The metrics once obtained are then used to compute Compliance and Product Visibility Score for the shelves and then forwared to compute the same for the whole store.

    The complaince is computed with regard to the Product Facings, the Planogram and the Visibility for the SKUs.

  • We allow the user with multiple functionalities to handle the results obtained.

    • Append to Store Dashboard
    • Copy to another Store
    • Forward to Mail
    • Discard Results

  • Store Dashboard

    Finally, a consolidated dashboard is obtained for the store reflecting the overall store performace over a period of time, in terms of compliance and the variation of the metrics for different brands and categories.
